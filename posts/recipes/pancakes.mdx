# Protein Pancakes

A quick and easy recipe for pancakes that are high in protein and full of flavor.

## Ingredients

- 1 scoop (30g) vanilla protein powder
- 1/2 cup (60g) oat flour (or finely blended oats)
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
- 1/2 cup (120ml) milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 1 large egg
- 1/2 banana, mashed
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- Cooking spray or a small amount of oil or butter

## Instructions

1. **Mix dry ingredients:** In a bowl, combine protein powder, oat flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
2. **Mix wet ingredients:** In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, mashed banana, and vanilla extract.
3. **Combine:** Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients. Mix gently until just combined.
4. **Cook:** Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease with cooking spray, oil, or butter.
5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake into the pan. Cook for 2–3 minutes until bubbles appear on the surface. Flip and cook for another 1–2 minutes until golden and cooked through.

## Serving Suggestions

- Serve with berries, yogurt, or a drizzle of honey or maple syrup.
- For extra protein, add a spoonful of Greek yogurt on top.
