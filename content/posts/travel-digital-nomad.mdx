Remote work has unlocked the ability to work from anywhere. Here are top destinations.

## Top Picks

### Lisbon, Portugal
- Great weather, affordable, strong wifi
- Thriving tech community
- Easy Schengen access

### Bali, Indonesia
- Low cost of living
- Beautiful coworking spaces
- Beach lifestyle

### Mexico City, Mexico
- Same timezone as US
- Rich culture, amazing food
- Growing startup scene

## Tips for Success

1. **Test Your Setup** - Ensure reliable internet
2. **Mind Time Zones** - Schedule around meetings
3. **Stay Legal** - Understand visa requirements
4. **Build Community** - Join local nomad groups
