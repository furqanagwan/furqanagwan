When you're deep in code, it's easy to forget about eating well. Meal prep is the solution.

## The Basics

1. **Pick a Prep Day** - Usually Sunday
2. **Plan Your Menu** - 3-4 recipes max
3. **Batch Cook** - Make large portions
4. **Store Properly** - Glass containers are best

## Easy Recipes

### Protein-Packed Bowls
- Base: Rice or quinoa
- Protein: Grilled chicken or tofu
- Veggies: Roasted or fresh
- Sauce: Make ahead in batches

### Overnight Oats
- Oats + milk + chia seeds
- Add fruit in the morning
- Endless flavor variations

Invest 2-3 hours weekly, save hours of decision fatigue.
